Recommended Attorneys for Professional Liability Case

Compiled: 2025-01-28

Case Requirements

  • Civil litigation (indemnización de perjuicios)
  • Professional liability / negligence experience
  • Understanding of tax law (27bis issue)
  • Located in Santiago or available nationally

Tier 1: Best Fit (Civil + Tax Expertise)

1. Schneider Abogados

Why: Has both Civil and Tributario departments. Explicitly handles "Indemnización de Perjuicios" cases.

  • Address: World Trade Center Santiago, Nueva Tajamar 481, Of. 2102, Torre Norte, Las Condes
  • Phone: +56 2 3267 1946
  • Website: https://schneiderabogados.cl
  • Civil: https://schneiderabogados.cl/abogados/civil/indemnización-de-perjuicios
  • Tax: https://schneiderabogados.cl/abogados/tributarios

Relevant expertise: - Responsabilidad civil contractual y extracontractual - Defensa ante SII - Planificación tributaria

2. Wolfenson Abogados

Why: Strong civil litigation practice including "Responsabilidad Civil e Indemnización de Perjuicios" and tax department.

  • Location: Las Condes, Santiago
  • Website: https://www.wolfenson.cl
  • Civil: https://www.wolfenson.cl/abogado-civilista-en-las-condes
  • Tax: https://www.wolfenson.cl/estudiodeabogados/abogado-tributario-chile

Relevant expertise: - Juicios de responsabilidad civil - Responsabilidad contractual y extracontractual - Asesoría tributaria y defensa ante SII

3. Legal Fiscal

Why: Specialized in tax law, useful for understanding the 27bis technical aspects.

  • Website: https://www.legalfiscal.cl
  • Team: Abogados + contadores auditores

Relevant expertise: - Derecho tributario especializado - Fiscalizaciones SII - Reclamaciones tributarias

Note: May need to partner with civil litigator for the negligence claim.

  • Tax litigation: https://www.beckerabogados.cl/abogados-juicios-tributarios/

  • Note: Likely expensive, better for large cases

Recommended Approach

  1. Initial consultation: Schneider or Wolfenson (have both civil + tax)
  2. Prepare: Case summary, transcript, damage calculation (~$28M CLP)
  3. Ask specifically:
  4. Experience with professional negligence cases
  5. Familiarity with Art. 27bis DL 825
  6. Fee structure (hourly vs. contingency)
  7. Estimated timeline and costs

What to Bring to Consultation

  1. This case file (04-CASE.md summary)
  2. Full transcript of Katherine Rebolledo consultation
  3. Property purchase documents (Dec 10, 2025)
  4. Any written communication with Katherine Rebolledo
  5. Damage calculation breakdown
  6. Evidence of what correct advice should have been (27bis research)

Cost Considerations

Estimated damages: ~$28M CLP (~$30K USD)

Litigation costs to consider: - Attorney fees (hourly or contingency) - Court costs - Expert witness (if needed) - Time investment

Questions to ask: - Do you offer contingency arrangements? - What's your estimate of success probability? - Expected timeline? - Total estimated costs?

Rule of thumb: If litigation costs exceed 30-40% of potential recovery, consider whether it's worth pursuing.
