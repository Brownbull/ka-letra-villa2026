Legal Research - 27bis IVA Recovery & Accountant Liability

The Core Issue: Article 27bis DL 825

Art. 27bis of DL 825 (Ley de IVA) allows companies to recover IVA on fixed asset purchases (including property) BEFORE generating taxable sales.

Key Requirement

The SpA/company must be the first buyer — purchasing directly from the inmobiliaria. If you buy as persona natural and transfer to SpA later, the 27bis benefit is LOST.

Why This Matters

  • Property: 3,681 UF (~140M CLP)
  • IVA component: ~22-27M CLP
  • This entire amount could have been recovered via 27bis
  • Accountant advised wrong timing → benefit lost

Evidence of Professional Negligence

What Makes This Case Strong

  1. Client asked the right questions — Claudia explicitly asked about:
  2. IVA recovery ("pedir el IVA de vuelta")
  3. Timing ("crear sociedad antes o después de comprar")

  4. Post-transfer recovery ("puedo pedir el crédito fiscal después")

  5. Accountant gave wrong answers:

  6. Said "generally buy first, transfer later" — WRONG for 27bis
  7. Said "sí" to post-transfer IVA recovery — MISLEADING

  8. Never mentioned 27bis at all

  9. Accountant admitted limitation:

  10. "No tenemos contadores inmobiliarios" — but still gave definitive advice

Standard of Care

A competent contador advising on property purchase for business use should know: - 27bis exists and how it works - Timing requirements for IVA recovery - When to refer to a specialist (which she admitted she should have but didn't)

Professional Liability Framework

Contractual Liability (Código Civil)

  • Arts. 1545-1559 — Breach of professional services contract
  • Must prove: contract existed, breach occurred, damages resulted

Extracontractual Liability

  • Arts. 2314-2334 — Negligent conduct causing harm
  • Must prove: negligence, damage, causation

Elements to Prove

  1. ✅ Duty — Contador had duty to advise correctly on tax matters
  2. ✅ Breach — Gave wrong advice on timing, misled on recovery
  3. ✅ Causation — Client followed advice, lost 27bis benefit
  4. ✅ Damages — ~20-27M CLP in unrecovered IVA

Colegio de Contadores

  • Ley 13.011 (1958) — Creates Colegio de Contadores de Chile
  • Has ethics code and disciplinary process
  • Can file complaint for professional misconduct
  • Note: Disciplinary action ≠ financial compensation
