Chilean Tax Advisory Application - Specification

Codename: TBD (ideas: TribuChile, ImpuestosAI, ContadorIA) Purpose: Help average Chileans navigate tax situations without relying on incompetent professionals

Vision

An AI-powered tool where anyone can: 1. Describe their situation (job loss, freelance income, property, etc.) 2. Get accurate, actionable tax advice based on Chilean law 3. Understand their rights and options 4. Know what documents they need 5. Calculate potential deductions/obligations

Target Users

  • People who lost their jobs (finiquito, cesantía)
  • Freelancers / boleta de honorarios workers
  • Property owners with tax questions
  • Small business owners
  • Anyone tired of paying accountants for vague answers

Core Use Cases (MVP)

1. Job Loss / Layoff

  • Finiquito taxation
  • Unemployment benefits (seguro de cesantía)
  • Deductible expenses during unemployment
  • Tax implications of severance packages

2. Freelance / Honorarios

  • Monthly PPM obligations
  • Annual tax return (F22)
  • Deductible expenses for independent workers
  • Boleta vs Factura decision

3. Property Related

  • Contribuciones
  • Rental income taxation
  • Property sale tax implications
  • First-home benefits

Technical Approach

  • Stack: TBD (likely React + Firebase similar to Boletapp, or Next.js)
  • AI Backend: Gemini or Claude for natural language understanding
  • Knowledge Base: Curated Chilean tax law database (SII regulations)
  • Sources: SII.cl, Código Tributario, relevant DFLs and laws

Key Laws to Index

  • Código Tributario (DL 830)
  • Ley de Impuesto a la Renta (DL 824)
  • Código del Trabajo (relevant sections)
  • Circulares SII
  • Relevant jurisprudence

Competitive Advantage

  • Free or low-cost (vs expensive accountants)
  • Available 24/7
  • Consistent, accurate answers based on actual law
  • Transparent reasoning (shows which law applies)
  • No "we'll see" bullshit

Development Phases

Phase 1: Research & Knowledge Base

  • Document common scenarios
  • Gather relevant laws and SII interpretations
  • Create structured knowledge base

Phase 2: MVP

  • Single use case (job loss scenario)
  • Basic Q&A interface
  • Reference to relevant laws

Phase 3: Expansion

  • Additional use cases
  • Document generation
  • Calculator tools
